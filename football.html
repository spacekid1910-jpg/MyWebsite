The role

The Busquets & Kamara mould

Signed for Ashville in 2022 at U12s level and have been a staple in the side ever since. The role is the deep-lying midfield anchor — screening the back four, recycling possession, and picking the moment to step into a tackle, in the mould of Sergio Busquets and Boubacar Kamara. When the squad's short at the back, right-back is the other spot that gets covered.
