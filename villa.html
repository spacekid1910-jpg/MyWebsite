The season that just happened

Europa League champions, 2026

The 2025–26 season was one to tell people about for years. Villa finished 4th in the Premier League under Unai Emery, then went all the way in the Europa League — beating Nottingham Forest over two legs in the semi-final before thumping SC Freiburg 3–0 in the final in Istanbul on 20 May 2026.

It was the club's first major trophy since the League Cup back in 1996, and only the second European title in Villa's history after the 1982 European Cup. A run of eleven straight wins across all competitions earlier in the season also equalled club records set in 1897 and 1914. The result sealed Champions League qualification for the new season too.
